#5 Slime rancher

Slime rancher is an indie game made by Monomi park, In which, you collect cute creatures known as Slimes, explore and unlock a vast alien ecosystem,
Unveil secrets on why the last owner of your farm left for an adventure.

Whats not to love! I have easily sank 600+ hours on this amazing game, and theres a great sequel, even better. 10/10



#4 Minecraft

You know it, we all love it, its Minecraft, does this even need an introduction, with a whopping 300 million copies sold, this open world sandbox is perfect for every players style, mine resources, build massive structures, fight mobs, beat the ender dragon, make redstone contraptions, play with friends, create all sorts of fun doohickeys and thingamajigs 10/10




#3 Satisfactory

Satisfactory is a game developed by Coffee stain studios, the same guys who made goat simulator. But unlike goat sim, you have to LOCK. IN. manufacture thousands of parts to make ONE thingy, Spend an hour trying to automate a product and get NOWHERE, Have a mental breakdown because you have to transport a product HALFWAY across the map, CRY because your powergrid shutdown because of one stutter in its supply line, Make a vacuum tube railcannon to launch yourself across the map at the speed of sound, to save a few seconds of travel time. I have over 100 hours on one savefile, and I havent even gotten half way...20/10 perfect game




#2 ULTRAKILL

"MANKIND IS DEAD. BLOOD IS FUEL. HELL IS FULL." those are the first lines in ULTRAKILL, they are impactful, it sends a brutal image to your mind, anyways, I'm getting ahead of myself. You play as V1, a warmachine that runs on blood as fuel, you are a prototype and the only one ever made, And your job is simple, keep yourself fueled, As you travese through the visceral layers of hell, shooting and tearing through hordes of enemies, taking in all the gore and blood you can. 10/10




#1 HELLDIVERS 2

Have you ever wanted to fight for democratic freedom, Well now you can with Arrowhead studios latest game, HELLDIVERS 2, Choose from an array of weapons, select 4 special weapons or tools to call in as needed known as stratagems, customize your characters armour to suit your playstyle, want to use napalm and light up the battlefield in a blaze of liberty, may i offer an armour set with 75 percent fire resistance, want to sneak around without being seen, choose an armour set that makes you practicaly invisible. Fight against those who seek to destroy managed democracy for good along side three other allies. This game cannot be rated as it is a perfect game
